Our Services

We provide high-quality imaging using state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment.

MRI – GE Signa Explorer 1.5T with Breast Coil

Use: High-resolution imaging for brain, spine, joints, abdomen & breast.

Prep: Usually none. Fasting may be needed for abdomen. Inform about implants.

CT Scan – GE Brivo 365

Use: Fast 3D imaging for brain, chest, abdomen, bones, trauma.

Prep: 4–6 hr fasting for contrast scans. Report allergies/kidney issues.

Ultrasound & Doppler – GE Voluson E10 / S8

Use: Obstetric, abdominal, pelvic, thyroid, testis, vascular studies.

Prep: Full bladder for pelvic. Fasting for upper abdomen.

Liver Elastography / Fat Assessment

Use: Non-invasive liver stiffness & fat measurement.

Prep: Fasting 3–4 hrs preferred. Quick & painless scan.

DEXA – Bone & Body Composition

Use: Bone density and fat/muscle analysis.

Prep: No calcium 24 hrs before. Light clothing preferred.

Digital X-ray & Mammography

Use: Chest/spine X-rays. Mammograms for breast screening.

Prep: No powder/deodorant before mammogram. Remove metal items.

Digital X-rays

Use: Routine imaging for limbs, chest, spine, etc.

Prep: No specific prep. Remove jewelry or metal items.

Samsung RS85 Ultrasound

Use: High-definition scanning for abdominal and vascular studies.

Prep: Fasting or full bladder as advised for your scan.
