🧠 MRI – GE Signa Explorer 1.5T with Breast Coil

What is MRI?

Magnetic Resonance Imaging uses powerful magnetic fields and radio waves to create highly detailed images of organs, soft tissues, bones, and internal body structures without ionizing radiation, making it exceptionally safe for repeated imaging.

Clinical Applications

  • • Brain & Spinal Cord imaging
  • • Joint evaluation (knee, shoulder, hip)
  • • Abdominal organ assessment
  • • Breast cancer screening
  • • Cardiac MRI studies
  • • Vascular imaging (MRA)

Preparation Guidelines

  • • Usually no dietary restrictions
  • • Fasting may be required for abdominal scans
  • • Remove all metal objects
  • • Inform staff about implants/pacemakers
  • • Arrive 30 minutes early

⚡ CT Scan – GE Brivo 365

Advanced Multi-Slice CT Technology

Our 64-128 slice CT scanner provides rapid, detailed cross-sectional imaging with exceptional clarity. Ideal for emergency situations, trauma evaluation, and comprehensive diagnostic assessment with advanced dose reduction technology.

Diagnostic Capabilities

  • • Emergency trauma assessment
  • • Chest & lung evaluation
  • • Abdominal organ imaging
  • • Brain & neurological studies
  • • CT Angiography (blood vessels)
  • • Cancer staging & monitoring

Preparation Requirements

  • • 4-6 hour fasting for contrast studies
  • • Report allergies & kidney issues
  • • Remove metal jewelry/objects
  • • Kidney function test if contrast needed
  • • Comfortable, loose clothing

🎗️ Digital Mammography & X-Ray

Advanced Breast Cancer Screening

Full-field digital mammography with tomosynthesis capabilities for early breast cancer detection. Low-dose radiation technology with auto-optimized compression for enhanced patient comfort and superior image quality.

Screening Applications

  • • Annual breast cancer screening
  • • Diagnostic mammography
  • • Breast biopsy guidance
  • • High-risk patient monitoring
  • • Digital chest X-rays
  • • Bone & joint X-rays

Preparation Guidelines

  • • No deodorant or powder before mammogram
  • • Remove all metal jewelry
  • • Two-piece clothing recommended
  • • Schedule after menstrual period
  • • Bring previous mammogram reports

🦴 DEXA – Bone & Body Composition Analysis

Precision Bone Density Measurement

Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry provides precise osteoporosis screening and bone health assessment. Low-dose radiation technology with WHO-standard guidelines for accurate mineral density measurement and fracture risk assessment.

Clinical Uses

  • • Osteoporosis screening
  • • Fracture risk assessment
  • • Body composition analysis
  • • Postmenopausal monitoring
  • • Steroid therapy monitoring
  • • Metabolic bone disease evaluation

Preparation Requirements

  • • No calcium supplements 24 hrs before
  • • Light, comfortable clothing
  • • Remove metal objects
  • • 5-30 minute scan duration
  • • No special dietary restrictions
